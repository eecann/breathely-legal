Privacy Policy

App: Breathely
Effective Date: 2026-02-20
Plain-English Summary
  • No accounts. We do not collect email, phone number, address, or location.
  • Most data stays on your device only (preferences, reminders, breathing stats).
  • Subscriptions are managed via RevenueCat on iOS (standard purchase/subscription data).
  • Notifications are local reminders scheduled on-device (no push token sent to our server).

1. Information We Store On Your Device (Not Sent to Our Servers)

Breathely stores certain information locally on your device using on-device storage (AsyncStorage) to provide core features (progress tracking, reminders, personalization). This data is not sent to our servers.

  • Preferences: favorites (exercise IDs), theme, language, haptics preference, optional user name.
  • Breathing & usage statistics: total exercises, total time, streak, last exercise date, last 14 days history, last 5 sessions.
  • Reminder settings: enabled/disabled, time, days of week, smart scheduling preferences.
  • Smart scheduling data: recent app open timestamps (last 7 days) used to suggest reminder times.

2. Third-Party Services

2.1 RevenueCat (Subscriptions / Purchases) — iOS only

If you purchase a subscription, Breathely uses RevenueCat to manage subscription status and purchase validation on iOS. We do not send a custom user ID (appUserID). RevenueCat processes standard SDK data such as device/app information and purchase/entitlement status.

RevenueCat Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

2.2 Expo Notifications (Local Reminders)

Breathely uses Expo Notifications only to schedule local reminder notifications on your device. We do not send push tokens or user identifiers to our servers. Notification permission status is processed on-device.

Expo Privacy: https://expo.dev/privacy

3. Information We Do Not Collect

  • Email, phone number, postal address
  • Account credentials (no login system)
  • Precise or approximate location data
  • Push tokens sent to our servers (we only use local notifications)
  • Advertising identifiers or ad networks
  • Analytics SDKs (e.g., Firebase Analytics, Facebook SDK)
  • Social login integrations

4. Data Retention & Deletion

On-device data remains on your device unless you delete the app or clear the app’s storage. Subscription data is handled by Apple and RevenueCat according to their policies.

5. Children’s Privacy

Breathely is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Effective Date” above will reflect the latest version.
